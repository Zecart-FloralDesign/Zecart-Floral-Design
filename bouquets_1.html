Substitution Policy

At times, our photos depict an overall theme or aesthetic that may feature a unique vase, which cannot be replicated precisely.

While your bouquet may vary slightly from the photo, its essence will remain true. Occasionally, substitutions of flowers and containers may be necessary due to weather, seasonal availability, and market conditions. Rest assured, we are committed to maintaining the style, theme, and color scheme of your arrangement, substituting only with items of equal or higher value.

If specific design elements are critical to your order, we encourage you to include them in the florist instructions during checkout or contact us directly to ensure their availability.
